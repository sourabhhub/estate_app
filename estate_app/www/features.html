Different Features






Cane CRM

H & T Contract Billing: - Manages billing processes for Haulage and Transport contracts.
Cane Billing: - Facilitates billing operations related to cane products.
Farmer Loan: - Manages loans and financial transactions related to farmers.
Agriculture Development: - Supports initiatives and processes for agricultural development.
Vendor Registration & Vehicle Registration: - Manages the registration and information of vendors.
Handles the registration and tracking of vehicles involved in agricultural operations.
Cane Weight: - Tracks and manages the weight of cane products.
Bank Master: - Manages information and transactions with banks, serving as a central repository for banking details.





Sugar ERP

Asset Management: - Tracks and manages organizational assets, covering identification, categorization, and location.
Accounting: - Offers a comprehensive accounting module for ledger management, journal entries, and financial reporting.
HR Payroll: - Manages human resources and payroll processes, including attendance, leave tracking, and automated payroll calculations.
Manufacturing: - Supports end-to-end manufacturing processes, from production planning to inventory control.
Buying and Selling: - Facilitates buying and selling processes within the organization.
Includes features for order management, invoicing, and customer/vendor interactions.
Inventory Management: - Manages and tracks inventory levels.
Includes features for stock monitoring, reorder management, and inventory valuation.















Mobile Application

Registration: - Enables the registration of farmers using the mobile application.
Cane Registration: - Facilitates the registration of cane products through the mobile platform.
Agriculture Development: - Supports agricultural development initiatives through mobile functionalities.
Trip Sheet: - Manages and records trip-related information for agricultural operations.
Employee Login & Logout: - Allows employees to log in and out of the mobile application.
Check-in & Checkout with Geolocation: - Tracks employee locations with geolocation during check-in and checkout processes.
Workflow for All Transactions: - Implements a streamlined workflow for managing and tracking all mobile-based transactions.
