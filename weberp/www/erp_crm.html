Features of ERP & CRM





ERP

Transparency in Transactions and Work: All transactions are instantly recorded on servers, allowing online reports to be accessible by both users and management. This creates openness and clarity in the entire process
Direct Farmer Interaction:On-Field Plantation Registration: Field staff directly register cane plantations on the land, and the actual registered plot area is accurately mapped using geotagging. This provides a face-to-face connection with farmers and precise location tracking.
Elimination of Duplicate Data Entry: The system reduces redundancy in plantation registration and field records. Field staff only need to input data once, and it is automatically saved on the server.
Transparent Vehicle Numbering System: Each cane transport vehicle is easily identifiable through RFID tags at designated points. Manual interventions are eliminated, enhancing transparency.
Analysis of Harvest to Crushing Time: The system enables analysis of the time taken from harvesting to crushing, aiding in efficient planning and operations.
Enhanced Control Over Field Staff: Daily visit reports and a GPS-based online attendance system ensure better supervision and management of field staff










CRM

Contact Management: Storing and managing customer contact details and related information.
Sales Automation: Streamlining and automating sales processes, from lead generation to deal closure.
Marketing Automation: Automating and optimizing marketing campaigns based on customer segmentation and behavior.
Customer Support: Providing tools for tracking and managing customer inquiries, complaints, and requests.
Analytics and Reporting: Generating insights from customer data to make informed business decisions.
Integration: Connecting CRM systems with other software and tools used by the business, such as email, e-commerce platforms, and customer service tools.
Mobile Access: Allowing users to access and update CRM data on mobile devices.
Workflow Automation: Automating routine tasks and processes to improve efficiency.
